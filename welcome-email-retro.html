A note from our co-founder

Welcome aboard — I'm really glad you're here.

We started Sogni because the future of creativity was quietly moving into a handful of giant data centers — metered by the credit, filtered by committee, and priced like a luxury good.

We built the opposite. Sogni runs on the Supernet — a decentralized GPU network powered by people, not data centers. That single choice is why your renders are cheaper, run in parallel by the hundreds, ship with the newest open models the week they drop, and stay on your terms — your models, your rules.

And it's not just open models anymore. You now also get the latest proprietary models — like Seedance 2.0 and GPT Image 2 — right alongside them, so the best of both worlds lives in one place: create across every Sogni app, or call it all straight from the SDK.

You don't need to think about any of that to use it. Just create. But it's the reason Sogni feels faster, cheaper, and freer than whatever you were using before. Can't wait to see what you make.

— Mauvis Ledford
Co-Founder & CEO, Sogni
